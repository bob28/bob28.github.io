Enactus Ontario Tech
I was the Project Lead of Skill Series, an Enactus Ontario Tech project that focuses on providing meaningful education on topics that are not covered in a standard university or college program. Furthermore, Skill Series focuses on bringing in guest speakers with industry experience to our workshops so that students can learn from professionals in the topic that we were covering. We had the universities' lead fitness representative at our healthy living workshop, and the CEO and Founder of CloudTax, a Canadian tax filing software, to talk at our tax workshop. As Project Lead, I led a team of 7 students to create 8 dynamic workshops which had over 700 viewers. I oversaw all aspects of Skill Series, from the marketing, budget, content, and competition talking points. Due to my application to the RBC Future Launch Project Accelerator, Skill Series was able to receive a grant of $3000 to cover our expenses. I worked with the team to create influential marketing ads that we ran on Facebook and Instagram to attract students to our project, and spoke directly to our guest speakers before and after our workshops. I had to manage my team effectively so that we could create informative and engaging videos for our YouTube channel and inspire students to pick up a new skill or hobby. On top of Project Lead, I also helped out at the Enactus regional and national competition where I assisted in directing and editing the presentation videos. I also taught a fellow Enactus student on how to edit videos so that they could take over my role in the future.
